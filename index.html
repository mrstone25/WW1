Weapons of World War I

Rifles Machine Guns Artillery Tanks Chemical Weapons

Rifles

The primary weapon for infantry soldiers, bolt-action rifles were widely used.

  • Lee-Enfield (UK)
  • Mauser Gewehr 98 (Germany)
  • Springfield M1903 (USA)

Machine Guns

Machine guns were devastating on the battlefield due to their rapid firepower.

  • Vickers (UK)
  • Maxim Gun (Germany)
  • Hotchkiss M1914 (France)

Artillery

Artillery caused the majority of battlefield deaths and shaped trench warfare.

  • Big Bertha (Germany)
  • Canon de 75 modèle 1897 (France)
  • BL 60-pounder (UK)

Tanks

Tanks were introduced in WWI to break through trenches and barbed wire defenses.

  • Mark I-IV (UK)
  • Sturmpanzerwagen A7V (Germany)
  • Renault FT (France)

Chemical Weapons

Chemical warfare, like chlorine and mustard gas, caused mass casualties and horror.

  • Chlorine Gas
  • Mustard Gas
  • Phosgene
